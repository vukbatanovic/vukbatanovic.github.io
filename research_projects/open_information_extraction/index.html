Open Information Extraction for the Slovenian and the Serbian Language

 
Go to Research Project Site


Open Information Extraction for the Slovenian and the Serbian Language is a two-year bilateral project between the Faculty of Computer and Information Science, University of Ljubljana and the School of Electrical Engineering, University of Belgrade. As a researcher on the project, I am tasked with the creation of the first dataset in Serbian annotated with coreference relations and with its subsequent use in the construction of the first coreference resolution system for Serbian. I am also working on the same task for Croatian.
